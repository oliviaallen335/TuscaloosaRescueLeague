What to expect

Your adoption packet

When you adopt, you'll receive:

  • Health records and vaccination history
  • Microchip registration information
  • Spay/neuter certificate (if applicable)
  • Care sheet with feeding and grooming guidelines
  • List of recommended supplies

Adoption process

  1. Browse our catalog and find a pet you're interested in
  2. Submit an adoption application online
  3. Our team reviews your application (1–3 business days)
  4. Schedule a meet-and-greet with the animal
  5. Complete adoption paperwork and pay the adoption fee
  6. Take your new pet home

Vet records

Each animal's vet records (vaccinations, treatments, health notes) are included in their adoption packet. You'll receive a copy to give to your veterinarian so they have the full history.
